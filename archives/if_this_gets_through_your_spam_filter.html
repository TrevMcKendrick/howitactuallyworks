The first American political affair (nyt article)

How the leading Democratic candidate in the 1988 presidential campaign abruptly lost & ended his career. Gary Hart is known as the guy who marks the beginning of politicians’ personal lives being fair game in the media and to voters.

Sex is Sex, But Money is Money (medium article, no explicit pictures but probably NSFW)

An incredible read about a Russian woman who moved to New York at 19 and worked as an escort for 5 years.

She considered herself an immigrant entrepreneur, and includes lessons likes “spend money to make money” and how to do pricing:

“I loved doing couples, but I charged more than twice as much. I got $2,000 an hour, and the sessions were usually at least two hours. I charged more not because the work was harder — it obviously wasn’t — but because I could. That’s the cool thing about capitalism.”
